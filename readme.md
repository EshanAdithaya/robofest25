Pin Connections for ESP32-WROOM-32:
Ultrasonic Sensors:
Front Ultrasonic:
- Trigger: GPIO 5
- Echo: GPIO 18

Left Ultrasonic:
- Trigger: GPIO 19
- Echo: GPIO 21

Right Ultrasonic:
- Trigger: GPIO 22
- Echo: GPIO 23

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Motors:

Left Motor:
- Forward: GPIO 25
- Backward: GPIO 26
- Enable: GPIO 12

Right Motor:
- Forward: GPIO 27
- Backward: GPIO 14
- Enable: GPIO 13

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Encoders:

Left Encoder:
- A: GPIO 32
- B: GPIO 33

Right Encoder:
- A: GPIO 34
- B: GPIO 35

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Other:

Success LED: GPIO 2
Mode Button: GPIO 0 (Built-in)
